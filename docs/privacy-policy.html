Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Meal Chat ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Meal Chat mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide (for example, login identifiers).
  • Meal log content, including text, photos, and nutrition estimates generated in-app.
  • Profile settings such as goals, body metrics, and preferences.
  • Subscription and purchase status required to provide paid features.
  • Technical and diagnostic data used for reliability, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide meal logging, nutrition estimation, dashboard, and report features.
  • To operate AI-based analysis and language support features.
  • To maintain app security, prevent abuse, and improve service quality.
  • To manage subscriptions, entitlements, and customer support requests.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited data with service providers that process data on our behalf, such as cloud infrastructure, analytics, payment, and AI processing providers. We do not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for app operation, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and security purposes.

6. Your Choices

  • You can request account-related support by contacting us.
  • You can remove the app at any time from your device.
  • You can manage subscription settings from your Apple ID account settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Meal Chat is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.
